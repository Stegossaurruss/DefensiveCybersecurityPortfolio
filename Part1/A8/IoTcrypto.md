## Discover cryptography used in Internet of Things devices

##### Google Home
- I have a Google Home in my place that I use to play music, control lights, and control my TV by voice
- I know it connects to wifi and communicates with my phone and voice, but after some research I discovered that it uses TLS encryption and uses certificates and keys to authenticate the connection with my phone.
- It also encrypts any voice commands (again with TLS) I give it to make sure my privacy is upheld.
- I discovered the cryptography by researching about my Google Home (an IoT device) and how Google uses cryptograph measures to uphold CIA.
