## Discover cryptography used in Internet of Things devices

##### Chromecast
- I have a chromecast in my place that I use to stream movies and TV shows to my ancient and outdated TV
- I know it connects to wifi and communicates with my phone, but after some research I discovered that it uses TLS encryption and uses certificates and keys to authenticate the connection with my phone.
- It almost always involves being connected to the same network, and then entering a code shown on the screen into my phone to initialise the connection.

- This is taken from https://support.google.com/product-documentation/answer/10231940, a Chromecast security review
