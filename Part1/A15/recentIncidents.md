## Discover 5 recent security incidents

##### Victorian Department of Education
- The organisation was hit by a major data breach that affected all of the government schools in Victoria.
- Attackers accessed personal information and passwords of past and present students.
- A clear breach of the confidentiality of all students involved
- Link: (https://www.cybernewscentre.com/15th-january-2026-cyber-update-victorian-schools-hit-by-major-data-breach/?ref=daily-cyber-update-newsletter)

##### Smile Orthodontics
- The business was targeted by hacker group SafePay, and much data was accessed and leaked from their systems.
- Data includes home addresses of staff, personal emails, medical certificates, payment plans and patient treatment histories.
- This data leak is another clear example of breach of confidentiality, with very sensitive information becoming available publically.
- Link: (https://www.cyberdaily.au/security/13304-exclusive-safepay-ransomware-claims-hack-of-smile-team-orthodontics?utm_source=newsletter&utm_campaign=Daily&utm_medium=email&utm_content=2026-03-11&utm_emailID=fe740e8223e19c88ab5a9844d8dad2669ae410a794b23cad1d082072530b5507)

##### Prosura
- A rental car insurance company, Prosura suffered a major data breach, and threat actors are selling the data on public forums.
- Data accessed includes names, contact details, policy information, travel destinations, drivers' licences of around 3000000 customers.
- Reportedly, Prosura did not meet the ransom demands of the attackers and the attackers started publishing the data.
- This is another clear example of financially motivated ransomware attacks that undermine the security of the Australian population.
- Link: (https://www.cybernewscentre.com/14-january-2026-prosura-breach-exposes-300k-customers/?ref=daily-cyber-update-newsletter)

##### Qantas
- In mid 2025, Qantas was targeted by hacker group Scattered Lapsus$ Hunters, and a breach occured through a 3rd party call centre.
- While more sensitive data like financial details and identity documents was not accessed, names, dates of birth and frequent flyer points were all subject to the breach.
- Working with groups like the Australia Federal Police to resolve it, this was another huge-scale breach of confidentiality.
- Link: (https://australiancybersecuritymagazine.com.au/stolen-qantas-customer-records-surface-on-dark-web/)

##### University of Mississippi Medical Centre
- THe UMMC was subjected to a ransomware attack and as a result, had to shut down clinic locations in Mississippi.
- The attack took down their IT systems and blocked access to their system of medical records, rendering everyday hospital services unavailable. Essential services were still active, but it took 9 days to regain use of their systems and longer to resolve the attack.
- This time ransomware was used to hinder the availability of the systems and data, putting lives in danger and leaving the 10000 UMMC employees without work for the duration.
- Link (https://www.bleepingcomputer.com/news/security/university-of-mississippi-medical-center-closes-clinics-after-ransomware-attack/)
