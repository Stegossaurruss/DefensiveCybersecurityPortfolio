## Discover 5 recent security incidents

##### Victorian Department of Education
- The organisation was hit by a major data breach that affected all of the government schools in Victoria.
- Attackers accessed personal information and passwords of past and present students.
- A clear breach of the confidentiality of all students involved
- Link: (https://www.cybernewscentre.com/15th-january-2026-cyber-update-victorian-schools-hit-by-major-data-breach/?ref=daily-cyber-update-newsletter)

##### Smile Orthodontics
- The business was targeted by hacker group SafePay, and much data was accessed and leaked from their systems.
- Data includes home addresses of staff, personal emails, medical certificates, payment plans and patient treatment histories.
- This data leak is another clear example of breach of confidentiality, with very sensitive information becoming available publically.
