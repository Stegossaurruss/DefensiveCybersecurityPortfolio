## Discover 5 AI-enabled security solutions

##### AI Fraud Detection
- AI based fraud detection has been implemented by many businesses like Paypal to analyse transaction data.
- Location, patterns of spending and behaviour of each user is tracked to identify if suspicious activity is detected.
- AI is used to learn what a normal purchase or transaction looks like and then flag and prevent unusual ones.
- I discovered this by researching the uses of AI in defensive cybersecurity
- Link: IBM (https://www.ibm.com/topics/fraud-detection)

##### Neural Networks with Facial Recognition
- Face ID and other facial recognition programs are commonly used as a security measure now, like using a facial scan to unlock a device.
- Many of these programs, such as Face ID, are powered by neural networks, an AI solution to security challenges like passwords.
- I discovered the use of neural networks through my knowledge of facial recognition and some confirming research.
- Link: Microsoft Azure (https://azure.microsoft.com/en-us/resources/cloud-computing-dictionary/what-is-face-recognition#:~:text=Face%20recognition%20is%20a%20form,identify%20or%20group%20a%20face.)
- Link: Apple (https://support.apple.com/en-in/102381)

##### AI Network Threat Detection - Darktrace
- Darktrace is cybersecurity tool that uses AI to detect unusual behaviour in a network.
- It does this by first learning what normal activity on the network looks like, then identifies anomalies.
- Because it has an image of "normal" behaviour, it can also detect zero-day exploits, very difficult to do without AI models.
- I discovered it by researching prominent and effective AI security tools in 2026.
- Link: Darktrace (https://www.darktrace.com/products/network)

##### AI System Protection - Cloudstrike Falcon
- Cloudstrike Falcon is another cybersecurity tool that focuses on protecting devices like computers and servers from threats.
- Like Darktrace, it monitors system activity and implements AI to identify suspicious or malicious behaviours.
- Depending on what platform and service is provided, it can also prevent threatening actions from being undertaken before they cause damage to the system it is protecting
- I discovered it again by research effective AI security tools.
- Link: Cloustrike Falcon (https://www.crowdstrike.com/en-us/platform/)

##### Automated Content Detection - Youtube
- Youtube uses automated content detection to indentify content that goes against their content policies and terms of service.
- This helps to better enforce their rules on a huge scale, and protect users from harmful, threatening or dangerous content.
- This applies to both videos and comments, and greatly helps to improve security on a platform as big as Youtube
- I discovered this through more research of AI-based security implementations, as I don't always see proof of AI usage on the surface.
- Link: Youtube (https://www.youtube.com/howyoutubeworks/our-policies/#:~:text=How%20Community%20&%20Ad%2DFriendly%20Guidelines,they're%20advertising%20on%20YouTube.)
