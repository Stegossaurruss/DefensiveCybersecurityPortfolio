## Security Concepts on Campus

##### 1. Campus Card Readers
- Card readers are used to provide access control to certain buildings, areas or resources on campus, a clear example of something a student could have to verift their identity.

##### 2. Eduroam Login and Security
- University wifi networks require student ID and password to login, and the data is secured with WPA2/WPA3 protocols. On top of that, there are other security measures on the network such as forced lockout after too much failed password attempts.
- I discovered this by thinking about university services that require a login.

##### 3. Security Cameras
- A very straightforward method, security cameras are used to protect sensitive areas and locations, so that suspicious/illegal behaviour can be detected and recordings can be checked in the case of a breach.
- I discoved this camera on the ground floor of Reid Library.
