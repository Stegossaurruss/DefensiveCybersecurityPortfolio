## Enhance the cybersecurity in your home

##### Password Manager
- My sister is a bit notorious for using the same password for everything, so I asked her if she would install a password manager.
- I did this with the goal of encouraging her to using varied, complex passwords for different applications, especially for important accounts that contain sensitive information like myGov, bank, Medicare and university accounts.
- I cannot confirm whether or not she will use the manager to actually implement diverse passwords, but I will continue to encourage her to enhance her cybersecurity measures, as well as use multi-factor authentication.
