## Discover 5 unique access control devices

##### Barcode
- Here I have a ticket for a festival in my Apple Wallet, and then barcode on it was scanned to allow entry into the festival.
- This is an example of access control using barcodes received upon payment, that permits access to an area for a service.
- I discovered it by buying the ticket and researching forms of access control.

##### RFID scan
- Here is another ticket I bought for a different festival, and instead of a barcode the ticket is in the Apple Wallet.
- This ticket uses NFC (Near Field Communication) similar to RFID to be scanned and matched against purchased tickets to permit entry.
- I discovered it again by buying the ticket and scanning it to access the event.

##### Location-based blocking
- Some websites, especially video and streaming services restrict some content based on country or location.
- Using a VPN I was watching a trailer only accessible in the USA and Canada, and upon turning off the VPN received this message.
- Access to the service is restricted based on geographic location.
<img width="498" height="160" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/263ae239-c04f-42af-a04f-20c58ddf38cb" />


##### Captcha
- Captcha is used to ensure that a human being is interacting with a site and not a bot.
- There are many different forms of captcha, but ReCaptcha is a very common protocol used.
- It restricts access to website and other services such as login by ensuring that only human users are permitted access.
<img width="335" height="105" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/2f61b659-9e15-41cf-a65f-0bae9bef458d" />


##### Netflix device access control
- Netflix lets you control which devices have access to your account, and which devices can remain logged in.
- I was able to access these controls from within my Netflix account, and logout and restrict access to the account from certain devices.
- I discovered this by researching parental controls in Netflix for this portfolio and instead finding these access controls.
