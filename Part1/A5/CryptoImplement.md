## Discover cryptographic implementation used online

##### Whatsapp End-to-end Encryption
- Whatsapp uses end-to-end encryption on messages sent, meaning even Whatsapp cannot see the content of your message. A message is encrypted on my device and then that is sent to the recipient, whose device can decrypt the message.
- Curve25519 is the public key algorithm used secure communications
- I discovered this by reseaching how social media platforms encrypt their data, and then navigating in the Whataspp mobile app.
