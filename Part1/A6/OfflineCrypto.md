##  Discover cryptographic implementation used offline.

##### FileVault on Mac
- Apple iOS has the option to enable FileVault, which is a method of encrypting the device's disk.
- It uses AES encryption to encrypt the entire hard drive when the device is locked, leaving it unreadable.
- When the device is unlocked, the password unlocks the decryption key.
- All of this happens while offline
