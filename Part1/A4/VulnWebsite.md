## Discover a vulnerable website

##### Bridge Association of WA
Website: http://www.bawa.asn.au/index.asp

- This website is vulnerable because it does not use HTTPS encryption, so any information input, sent or received by the site is vulnerable to eavesdropping, interception, modification and other threats. As well as this confidentiality and integrity are broken because your information is accessible by others, and it is possible to modify it through something like a man in the middle attack.
- I discovered this website through searching for vulnerable websites, browsing through lists of clubs and associations in Perth.
